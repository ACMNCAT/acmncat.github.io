Welcome

Welcome to the online home of Association for Computing Machinery at North Carolina Agricultural & Technical State University. We are the premier computing and technology community at NC A&T. We are a community of students, falculty, professionals, and alumni. Throughout the year we hold various events and programs. Our events include general body meetings, company workshops, social events, and hackathons. All events for Fall 2020 will be held virtually. Our Special Interest Groups or SIGs are a unique highlight of our organization. Computing and technology are very broad fields. SIGs are a way that members can explore and get more involved with their interests. ACM is also a community that gives back to the community. Members are involved with community service on campus and around Greensboro.
